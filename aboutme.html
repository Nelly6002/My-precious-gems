About Me

My name is Lydia, and I am a humble proud mother of three lovely children. I have worked in Early Years since 2018 as a Nursery Practitioner and established My Precious Gems in 2021.

I hold a Level 3 Qualification in Childcare and Education and currently serve as the Designated safeguarding lead of the setting.

I am First Aid trained with experience working with children with SEND. I am Ofsted registered with a Good rating.

I also hold certifications in Food Hygiene (Level 2), Safeguarding, and Prevent Training. I am committed to continuous professional development to remain competent in providing safe, quality childcare.
